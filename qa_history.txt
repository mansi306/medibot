Q [en]: how to cure fever 
A [en]: To manage fever, both non-pharmacological and pharmacological treatments can be employed. Non-pharmacologically, hydrotherapy and rest are advised. Pharmacologically, over-the-counter medications containing aspirin or salicylates should be avoided. Instead, Paracetamol tablets (500-1000mg every 6-8 hours) or Ibuprofen tablets (400-600mg every 8 hours) can be used for general relief. For more specific situations like suspected bacterial or malarial causes based on clinical and lab evaluations, antibiotics or antimalarials might be prescribed respectively. It's crucial to consult a healthcare provider before self-medicating.
